## The Truth (DM Eyes Only)

Fifteen years ago, a warlock named Voss came to Salthollow, dying of a curse he'd earned from a dark patron. Elara, the village healer, tried to save him. As Voss died, his patron's curse leapt to Elara — twisting her over weeks into something inhuman. The villagers, led by Elder Sera and with Thessa's reluctant agreement, drove Elara out. She fled to the sea caves. Over the years, the curse completed its work, transforming her into the sea hag Maren. She discovered that tears of genuine remorse from those who wronged her could break the curse — but the villagers refused to acknowledge what they'd done. So she began taking them, placing them in magical stasis, trying to force their tears through enchanted dreams of guilt. It hasn't worked. The stasis keeps them too deeply asleep to truly weep.
