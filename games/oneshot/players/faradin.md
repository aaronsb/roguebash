## Racial Traits (Lightfoot Halfling)
- **Lucky**: Reroll natural 1s on attack rolls, ability checks, and saving throws. Must use the new roll.
- **Brave**: Advantage on saving throws against being frightened.
- **Halfling Nimbleness**: Move through the space of any creature of Medium size or larger.
- **Naturally Stealthy**: Can attempt to hide when obscured only by a creature at least one size larger.

## Class Features (Ranger 3 — Hunter)

### Favored Enemy: Beasts
- Advantage on WIS (Survival) checks to track beasts
- Advantage on INT checks to recall information about beasts
- Bonus language: Sylvan

### Natural Explorer: Coast
While in coastal terrain:
- Difficult terrain doesn't slow the group
- Can't become lost except by magical means
- Remain alert to danger even when doing other activities
- Can move stealthily at normal pace when traveling alone
- Find twice as much food when foraging
- While tracking, learn exact number, sizes, and how long ago they passed
- Double proficiency bonus on INT/WIS checks using proficient skills

### Fighting Style: Archery
+2 bonus to attack rolls with ranged weapons (included in shortbow attack).

### Spellcasting
- **Spell Save DC**: 12
- **Spell Attack Bonus**: +4
- **Spell Slots**: 3 first-level (3/3 remaining)

### Spells Known (3)
1. **Hunter's Mark** (1st, concentration, 1 hour) — Bonus action to mark a creature. Deal extra 1d6 damage on each hit. Advantage on Survival/Perception to find it. Bonus action to move mark when target drops to 0 HP.
2. **Cure Wounds** (1st, touch) — Restore 1d8+2 HP to a creature you touch.
3. **Goodberry** (1st, instantaneous) — Create 10 berries. Each heals 1 HP when eaten. Last 24 hours.

### Hunter's Prey: Colossus Slayer
Once per turn, when you hit a creature with a weapon attack, it takes an extra 1d8 damage if it's below its hit point maximum.

## Background: Wayfarer
**Feature — Kindness of Strangers**: In most settlements, Faradin can find a place to sleep and a meal by offering honest labor — fixing a fence, mending a chair, sharpening tools. Common folk are inclined to help a polite, hardworking halfling.

**Tool Proficiency**: Carpenter's tools
**Language**: Sylvan (rolled into Favored Enemy language)

## Personality
- **Trait**: Easy-going, observant, self-deprecating. The kind of fellow you'd invite in for supper without quite knowing why.
- **Ideal**: *Freedom* — the road provides, as long as you keep walking.
- **Bond**: Left his halfling village young. Sends coin home when he can, though he's not sure it arrives.
- **Flaw**: Always has one foot out the door. Getting attached means getting stuck.

## Appearance
Small even for a halfling, with sun-darkened skin and laugh lines around hazel eyes. His curly brown hair is perpetually windswept. Wears a patched traveling cloak over well-worn leathers, with various tools and trinkets hanging from his belt. His hands are rough from work but nimble from years of carving and fixing.
