# oneshot/ — The Vanishing Tide

A self-contained one-shot mystery/horror adventure for a single level-3 player character, set in the fog-shrouded coastal village of Salthollow. Designed to be completed in a single session (3-5 hours).

## Adventure Summary

Villagers are vanishing from Salthollow, taken one by one from the shoreline during storms. The player arrives seeking shelter on a stormy night and gets drawn into the mystery. Investigation leads to the sea caves below the old lighthouse, where the truth waits: the "monster" is a sea hag named Maren — once a beloved village healer called Elara, cursed and cast out by the very people she tried to protect. She hasn't killed the missing villagers. They sleep in magical stasis while she works to break her curse. The player must choose how to end this.

## Tone and Themes

- **Genre**: Mystery/horror with moral complexity
- **Atmosphere**: Fog, cold rain, crashing waves, whispered fears, candlelit rooms
- **Central tension**: Appearances deceive. The villagers are not entirely innocent. The monster is not entirely monstrous.
- **Player agency**: Multiple valid endings. No single "correct" choice.

## Running This One-Shot

1. Have the player create or bring a level 3 character
2. Use `/session start` or read `scenario.md` for the adventure flow
3. Brine serves as a companion NPC to support the solo player in combat
4. Track time in `calendar.md` — the adventure spans roughly one night and one morning
5. The adventure is structured in 4 acts but should flow naturally based on player choices
