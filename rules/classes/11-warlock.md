# Warlock — The Fiend

## Class Basics
- **Hit Die**: d8
- **HP at 1st Level**: 8 + CON modifier
- **HP at Higher Levels**: 1d8 (or 5) + CON modifier per level
- **Primary Ability**: Charisma
- **Saving Throws**: Wisdom, Charisma
- **Armor**: Light armor
- **Weapons**: Simple weapons
- **Skills**: Choose 2 from Arcana, Deception, History, Intimidation, Investigation, Nature, Religion

## Pact Magic
Warlock spellcasting works differently from other casters:
- **Ability**: Charisma
- **Spell Save DC**: 8 + proficiency bonus + CHA modifier
- **Spell Attack**: proficiency bonus + CHA modifier
- **All spell slots are the same level** — they scale up together
- **Spell slots recharge on a SHORT or long rest** (unlike other casters)
- **Spells known** (not prepared)

## Class Features

### Otherworldly Patron (1st Level)
Choose The Fiend (SRD subclass).

### Eldritch Invocations (2nd Level)
You gain two eldritch invocations. You gain additional invocations as shown in the table. When you gain a level, you can replace one invocation with another you qualify for.

### Pact Boon (3rd Level)
Choose one:

**Pact of the Chain.** You learn Find Familiar (doesn't count against spells known). Your familiar can take one of the following special forms: imp, pseudodragon, quasit, or sprite. When you take the Attack action, you can forgo one attack to allow your familiar to use its reaction to make one attack.

**Pact of the Blade.** You can use your action to create a pact weapon in your empty hand. You choose the form (any melee weapon). You are proficient with it. It counts as magical. It disappears if it is more than 5 feet from you for 1 minute. You can transform one magic weapon into your pact weapon by performing a 1-hour ritual.

**Pact of the Tome.** You receive a Book of Shadows. Choose three cantrips from any class's spell list. While the book is in your possession, you can cast those cantrips at will.

### Ability Score Improvement
Levels 4, 8, 12, 16, 19.

### Mystic Arcanum (11th, 13th, 15th, 17th Level)
You gain one spell of each level as an arcanum:
- **11th**: One 6th-level spell
- **13th**: One 7th-level spell
- **15th**: One 8th-level spell
- **17th**: One 9th-level spell

Each can be cast once per long rest without expending a spell slot.

### Eldritch Master (20th Level)
You can spend 1 minute entreating your patron to regain all your expended Pact Magic spell slots. Once per long rest.

## The Fiend Features

### Expanded Spell List
You gain access to additional spells at each spell level:

| Spell Level | Spells |
|------------|--------|
| 1st | Burning Hands, Command |
| 2nd | Blindness/Deafness, Scorching Ray |
| 3rd | Fireball, Stinking Cloud |
| 4th | Fire Shield, Wall of Fire |
| 5th | Flame Strike, Hallow |

### Dark One's Blessing (1st Level)
When you reduce a hostile creature to 0 hit points, you gain **temporary hit points equal to your CHA modifier + your warlock level** (minimum 1).

### Dark One's Own Luck (6th Level)
When you make an ability check or a saving throw, you can add **a d10** to your roll. You can do so after seeing the initial roll but before any effects. Once per short or long rest.

### Fiendish Resilience (10th Level)
Choose one damage type when you finish a short or long rest. You gain **resistance** to that damage type until you choose a different one. Damage from magical weapons or silver weapons ignores this resistance.

### Hurl Through Hell (14th Level)
When you hit a creature with an attack, you can instantly transport the target through the lower planes. The creature disappears and hurtles through a nightmarish landscape. At the end of your next turn, it returns to the space it previously occupied (or the nearest unoccupied space). If the target is not a fiend, it takes **10d10 psychic damage** as it reels from its horrific experience. Once per long rest.
