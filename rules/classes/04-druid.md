# Druid — Circle of the Land

## Class Basics
- **Hit Die**: d8
- **HP at 1st Level**: 8 + CON modifier
- **HP at Higher Levels**: 1d8 (or 5) + CON modifier per level
- **Primary Ability**: Wisdom
- **Saving Throws**: Intelligence, Wisdom
- **Armor**: Light armor, medium armor, shields (druids will not wear armor or use shields made of metal)
- **Weapons**: Clubs, daggers, darts, javelins, maces, quarterstaffs, scimitars, sickles, slings, spears
- **Tools**: Herbalism kit
- **Skills**: Choose 2 from Arcana, Animal Handling, Insight, Medicine, Nature, Perception, Religion, Survival

## Spellcasting
- **Ability**: Wisdom
- **Spell Save DC**: 8 + proficiency bonus + WIS modifier
- **Spell Attack**: proficiency bonus + WIS modifier
- **Prepared Spells**: WIS modifier + druid level (minimum 1)
- **Ritual Casting**: Yes (if spell prepared)
- **Focus**: Druidic focus (sprig of mistletoe, totem, wooden staff, yew wand)
- **Cantrips Known**: 2 at 1st, 3 at 4th, 4 at 10th

Uses the full caster spell slot table (see spellcasting/01-spellcasting-rules.md).

## Class Features

### Druidic (1st Level)
You know Druidic, the secret language of druids. You can speak the language and use it to leave hidden messages.

### Wild Shape (2nd Level)
As an action, magically assume the shape of a beast you have seen before. You can use this feature **twice per short or long rest**.

**Beast Limitations by Level:**

| Level | Max CR | Limitations | Example |
|-------|--------|-------------|---------|
| 2nd | 1/4 | No flying or swimming speed | Wolf |
| 4th | 1/2 | No flying speed | Crocodile |
| 8th | 1 | No limitations | Giant Eagle |

**Duration**: Half your druid level (rounded down) hours.

**Wild Shape Rules**:
- Your game statistics are replaced by the beast's, but you retain alignment, personality, INT/WIS/CHA scores
- You retain skill and saving throw proficiencies (use beast's if higher)
- You can't cast spells in beast form (until Beast Spells at 18th)
- You assume the beast's HP. When you revert, you return to your pre-transformation HP. Excess damage carries over.
- You choose whether equipment falls, merges, or is worn by beast form
- You revert if you drop to 0 HP, fall unconscious, or choose to (bonus action)

### Druid Circle (2nd Level)
Choose Circle of the Land (SRD subclass).

### Ability Score Improvement
Levels 4, 8, 12, 16, 19.

### Timeless Body (18th Level)
For every 10 years that pass, your body ages only 1 year.

### Beast Spells (18th Level)
You can cast spells in Wild Shape form. You can perform somatic and verbal components, but can't provide material components that have a cost or are consumed.

### Archdruid (20th Level)
You can use Wild Shape an unlimited number of times. You can ignore verbal and somatic components of druid spells, and material components without a cost that aren't consumed.

## Circle of the Land Features

### Bonus Cantrip (2nd Level)
You learn one additional druid cantrip of your choice.

### Natural Recovery (2nd Level)
During a short rest, you can recover expended spell slots with a combined level equal to or less than half your druid level (rounded up), and none of the slots can be 6th level or higher. Once per long rest.

### Land's Stride (6th Level)
Moving through nonmagical difficult terrain costs no extra movement. You can pass through nonmagical plants without being slowed or taking damage. Advantage on saves against magically created or manipulated plants (e.g., Entangle).

### Nature's Ward (10th Level)
You can't be charmed or frightened by elementals or fey, and you are immune to poison and disease.

### Nature's Sanctuary (14th Level)
Beasts and plant creatures that attack you must make a WIS saving throw against your spell save DC. On a failed save, the creature must choose a different target or the attack misses. On a success, the creature is immune to this effect for 24 hours.
