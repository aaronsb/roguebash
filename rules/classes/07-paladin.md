# Paladin — Oath of Devotion

## Class Basics
- **Hit Die**: d10
- **HP at 1st Level**: 10 + CON modifier
- **HP at Higher Levels**: 1d10 (or 6) + CON modifier per level
- **Primary Ability**: Strength and Charisma
- **Saving Throws**: Wisdom, Charisma
- **Armor**: All armor, shields
- **Weapons**: Simple weapons, martial weapons
- **Skills**: Choose 2 from Athletics, Insight, Intimidation, Medicine, Persuasion, Religion

## Spellcasting (2nd Level)
- **Ability**: Charisma
- **Spell Save DC**: 8 + proficiency bonus + CHA modifier
- **Spell Attack**: proficiency bonus + CHA modifier
- **Prepared Spells**: CHA modifier + half paladin level (rounded down), minimum 1
- **Focus**: Holy symbol

## Class Features

### Divine Sense (1st Level)
As an action, you can detect the location of any celestial, fiend, or undead within 60 feet that is not behind total cover. You also detect the presence of any place or object that has been consecrated or desecrated. You can use this feature **1 + CHA modifier** times per long rest.

### Lay on Hands (1st Level)
You have a pool of healing power equal to **paladin level x 5** HP. As an action, touch a creature and restore any number of HP from your pool. Alternatively, expend 5 HP from the pool to cure one disease or neutralize one poison. No effect on undead or constructs. Pool replenishes on long rest.

### Fighting Style (2nd Level)
Choose one: Defense (+1 AC in armor), Dueling (+2 damage one-handed), Great Weapon Fighting (reroll 1-2 on two-handed damage), Protection (reaction: disadvantage on attack vs. ally within 5 ft., requires shield).

### Divine Smite (2nd Level)
When you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack, you can expend one spell slot to deal extra radiant damage: **2d8 for a 1st-level slot, plus 1d8 for each slot level above 1st** (maximum 5d8). The damage increases by **1d8 if the target is an undead or a fiend**.

### Divine Health (3rd Level)
You are immune to disease.

### Sacred Oath (3rd Level)
Choose Oath of Devotion (SRD subclass).

### Extra Attack (5th Level)
Two attacks when you take the Attack action.

### Aura of Protection (6th Level)
Whenever you or a friendly creature within **10 feet** of you must make a saving throw, the creature gains a bonus equal to your **Charisma modifier** (minimum +1). You must be conscious. At **18th level**, the range increases to **30 feet**.

### Aura of Courage (10th Level)
You and friendly creatures within **10 feet** of you can't be frightened while you are conscious. At **18th level**, the range increases to **30 feet**.

### Improved Divine Smite (11th Level)
Whenever you hit a creature with a melee weapon, the creature takes an extra **1d8 radiant damage**. This is automatic — no spell slot required.

### Cleansing Touch (14th Level)
As an action, you can end one spell on yourself or on one willing creature that you touch. You can use this feature **CHA modifier** times per long rest (minimum once).

### ASI
Levels 4, 8, 12, 16, 19.

## Oath of Devotion Features

### Tenets of Devotion
- **Honesty**: Don't lie or cheat
- **Courage**: Never fear to act
- **Compassion**: Aid others, protect the weak
- **Honor**: Treat others with fairness
- **Duty**: Be responsible for your actions and their consequences

### Channel Divinity (3rd Level)
One use per short or long rest. Choose one effect:

**Sacred Weapon.** As an action, imbue one weapon you're holding with positive energy for 1 minute. You add your **CHA modifier** to attack rolls (minimum +1). The weapon emits bright light in a 20-foot radius and dim light 20 feet beyond. If not already magical, it becomes magical. You can end this as a free action, or if you are no longer holding the weapon.

**Turn the Unholy.** As an action, each fiend or undead within 30 feet must make a **WIS saving throw**. On failure, it is turned for 1 minute or until it takes damage. Turned creatures must Dash away from you and can't willingly move within 30 feet of you, and can't take reactions.

### Aura of Devotion (7th Level)
You and friendly creatures within **10 feet** can't be charmed while you are conscious. At **18th level**, the range increases to **30 feet**.

### Purity of Spirit (15th Level)
You are always under the effects of Protection from Evil and Good.

### Holy Nimbus (20th Level)
As an action, you emanate an aura of sunlight for 1 minute. You emit bright light in a 30-foot radius and dim light 30 feet beyond. Whenever an enemy creature starts its turn in the bright light, it takes **10 radiant damage**. You have advantage on saving throws against spells cast by fiends or undead. Once per long rest.
