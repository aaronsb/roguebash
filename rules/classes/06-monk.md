# Monk — Way of the Open Hand

## Class Basics
- **Hit Die**: d8
- **HP at 1st Level**: 8 + CON modifier
- **HP at Higher Levels**: 1d8 (or 5) + CON modifier per level
- **Primary Ability**: Dexterity and Wisdom
- **Saving Throws**: Strength, Dexterity
- **Armor**: None
- **Weapons**: Simple weapons, shortswords
- **Tools**: Choose one type of artisan's tools or one musical instrument
- **Skills**: Choose 2 from Acrobatics, Athletics, History, Insight, Religion, Stealth

## Class Features

### Unarmored Defense (1st Level)
While wearing no armor and not wielding a shield, your AC equals **10 + DEX modifier + WIS modifier**.

### Martial Arts (1st Level)
While unarmed or wielding only monk weapons (shortswords and simple melee weapons without heavy or two-handed) and not wearing armor or a shield:
- You can use DEX instead of STR for attack and damage rolls of unarmed strikes and monk weapons
- You can roll the Martial Arts die (see table) in place of normal damage
- When you take the Attack action with an unarmed strike or monk weapon, you can make one unarmed strike as a bonus action

### Ki (2nd Level)
Ki points equal your monk level. **Ki save DC = 8 + proficiency bonus + WIS modifier**. Regain all ki on a short or long rest.

**Ki abilities:**
- **Flurry of Blows** (1 ki): Immediately after you take the Attack action, you can make two unarmed strikes as a bonus action.
- **Patient Defense** (1 ki): You can take the Dodge action as a bonus action.
- **Step of the Wind** (1 ki): You can take the Disengage or Dash action as a bonus action, and your jump distance is doubled for the turn.

### Unarmored Movement (2nd Level)
Your speed increases while not wearing armor or wielding a shield (see table). At 9th level, you can move along vertical surfaces and across liquids on your turn without falling during the move.

### Deflect Missiles (3rd Level)
As a reaction when hit by a ranged weapon attack, reduce the damage by **1d10 + DEX modifier + monk level**. If you reduce the damage to 0, you can catch the missile and spend 1 ki to make a ranged attack with it (range 20/60, counts as a monk weapon).

### Slow Fall (4th Level)
As a reaction when you fall, reduce falling damage by **5 x your monk level**.

### Extra Attack (5th Level)
Two attacks when you take the Attack action.

### Stunning Strike (5th Level)
When you hit with a melee weapon attack, you can spend 1 ki point. The target must succeed on a **CON saving throw** or be **stunned** until the end of your next turn.

### Ki-Empowered Strikes (6th Level)
Your unarmed strikes count as magical for overcoming resistance and immunity.

### Evasion (7th Level)
When subjected to an effect that allows a DEX save for half damage: success = no damage, failure = half damage.

### Stillness of Mind (7th Level)
You can use your action to end one effect causing you to be charmed or frightened.

### Purity of Body (10th Level)
You are immune to disease and poison.

### Tongue of the Sun and Moon (13th Level)
You understand all spoken languages. Any creature that can understand a language can understand what you say.

### Diamond Soul (14th Level)
You gain proficiency in all saving throws. Additionally, you can spend 1 ki to reroll a failed saving throw and must take the second result.

### Timeless Body (15th Level)
You don't suffer the frailty of old age and can't be aged magically. You no longer need food or water.

### Empty Body (18th Level)
You can spend 4 ki points to become invisible for 1 minute. During that time, you also have resistance to all damage except force. You can spend 8 ki points to cast the Astral Projection spell without needing material components (yourself only).

### Perfect Self (20th Level)
When you roll initiative and have no ki points, you regain 4 ki points.

## Way of the Open Hand Features

### Open Hand Technique (3rd Level)
Whenever you hit a creature with a Flurry of Blows attack, you can impose one of these effects (no ki cost):
- **Knock Prone**: Target must succeed on a **DEX saving throw** or be knocked prone
- **Push**: Target must succeed on a **STR saving throw** or be pushed up to 15 feet away
- **No Reactions**: Target can't take reactions until the end of your next turn

### Wholeness of Body (6th Level)
As an action, you can regain hit points equal to **three times your monk level**. Once per long rest.

### Tranquility (11th Level)
At the end of a long rest, you gain the effect of a Sanctuary spell that lasts until the start of your next long rest (the spell can end early as normal). The saving throw DC = **8 + WIS modifier + proficiency bonus**.

### Quivering Palm (17th Level)
When you hit a creature with an unarmed strike, you can spend 3 ki points to start imperceptible vibrations in the target's body, which last for a number of days equal to your monk level. You can have only one creature under this effect at a time. When you use an action to end the vibrations, the creature must make a **CON saving throw**. On a failure, it is reduced to **0 hit points**. On a success, it takes **10d10 necrotic damage**.
