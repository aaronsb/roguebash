## Actions in Combat

| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Attack** | Make melee or ranged attack(s) |
| **Cast a Spell** | Cast a spell with 1 action casting time |
| **Dash** | Double your movement for the turn |
| **Disengage** | Movement doesn't provoke opportunity attacks |
| **Dodge** | Attacks against you have disadvantage; DEX saves have advantage |
| **Help** | Give an ally advantage on their next check or attack |
| **Hide** | Make a Stealth check to become hidden |
| **Ready** | Prepare action with trigger (uses reaction to execute) |
| **Search** | Make Perception or Investigation check |
| **Use an Object** | Interact with a second object this turn |

## Conditions Quick Reference

| Condition | Key Effects |
|-----------|------------|
| **Blinded** | Auto-fail sight checks; attacks have disadv; attacks against have adv |
| **Charmed** | Can't attack charmer; charmer has adv on social checks |
| **Deafened** | Auto-fail hearing checks |
| **Frightened** | Disadv on checks/attacks while source visible; can't willingly approach |
| **Grappled** | Speed 0; ends if grappler incapacitated or effect removes you |
| **Incapacitated** | Can't take actions or reactions |
| **Invisible** | Attacks have adv; attacks against have disadv |
| **Paralyzed** | Incapacitated; auto-fail STR/DEX saves; attacks have adv; melee hits are crits |
| **Petrified** | Turned to stone; weight x10; resistance all damage; immune poison/disease |
| **Poisoned** | Disadv on attack rolls and ability checks |
| **Prone** | Disadv on attacks; melee attacks against have adv; ranged against have disadv |
| **Restrained** | Speed 0; attacks have disadv; DEX saves have disadv; attacks against have adv |
| **Stunned** | Incapacitated; auto-fail STR/DEX saves; attacks against have adv |
| **Unconscious** | Incapacitated; drop held items; prone; auto-fail STR/DEX; attacks have adv; melee crits |

## Exhaustion

| Level | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| 1 | Disadvantage on ability checks |
| 2 | Speed halved |
| 3 | Disadvantage on attack rolls and saving throws |
| 4 | Hit point maximum halved |
| 5 | Speed reduced to 0 |
| 6 | Death |

Effects are cumulative. One level removed per long rest with food and water.

## Death Saving Throws

- Roll d20 at start of each turn while at 0 HP
- 10+ = success, 9 or below = failure
- Natural 20 = regain 1 HP and become conscious
- Natural 1 = two failures
- 3 successes = stable (unconscious but not dying)
- 3 failures = death
- Any healing = conscious at new HP total
- Taking damage = 1 failure (crit from melee within 5 ft = 2 failures)

## Concentration

- Only one concentration spell at a time
- CON save when taking damage: DC = 10 or half damage taken (whichever is higher)
- Ends if: incapacitated, killed, cast another concentration spell, or choose to end
