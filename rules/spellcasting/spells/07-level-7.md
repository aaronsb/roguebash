# 7th-Level Spells

## Arcane Sword
*7th-level evocation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 60 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (a miniature platinum sword with a grip and pommel of copper and zinc, worth 250 gp) | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
You create a sword-shaped plane of force that hovers within range. When it appears, you make a melee spell attack against a target within 5 feet of the sword: **3d10 force** on hit. Until the spell ends, you can use a bonus action on each turn to move the sword up to 20 feet and repeat the attack.

## Conjure Celestial
*7th-level conjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 1 minute | **Range:** 90 feet | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour
You summon a celestial of CR 4 or lower. It is friendly to you and your companions. It acts on its own initiative and obeys your verbal commands. If your concentration is broken, the celestial doesn't disappear but becomes uncontrolled. It disappears when it drops to 0 HP or the spell ends. **At Higher Levels.** 9th: CR 5 or lower.

## Delayed Blast Fireball
*7th-level evocation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 150 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (a tiny ball of bat guano and sulfur) | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
A beam of yellow light flashes from your pointing finger, then condenses to linger at a chosen point within range as a glowing bead. When the spell ends (by your choice, losing concentration, or when a creature touches it), the bead detonates in a 20-foot-radius sphere: DEX save, **12d6 fire** (half on success). The fire damage increases by **1d6 for each round you delay** detonation, to a maximum of an additional 5d6. **At Higher Levels.** +1d6 base per slot above 7th.

## Divine Word
*7th-level evocation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 bonus action | **Range:** 30 feet | **Components:** V | **Duration:** Instantaneous
You utter a divine word imbued with the power that shaped the world. Each creature of your choice that can hear you within range must make a CHA save. Effects based on current HP: **50+ HP**: deafened 1 minute. **40 or fewer**: deafened and blinded 10 minutes. **30 or fewer**: blinded, deafened, and stunned 1 hour. **20 or fewer**: killed instantly. Regardless of HP, a celestial, elemental, fey, or fiend that fails is forced back to its home plane (can't return for 24 hours except by Wish).

## Etherealness
*7th-level transmutation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** Self | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Up to 8 hours
You step into the border regions of the Ethereal Plane, in the area where it overlaps with your current plane. You remain in the Border Ethereal for the duration or until you use your action to dismiss the spell. You can see and hear the plane you originated from (gray, 60 feet visibility). You can move in any direction and pass through solid objects on the Material Plane. You can only affect and be affected by other creatures on the Ethereal Plane. **At Higher Levels.** One additional creature per slot above 7th.

## Finger of Death
*7th-level necromancy*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 60 feet | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Instantaneous
You send negative energy coursing through a creature. The target must make a CON save: **7d8+30 necrotic** on failure, half on success. A humanoid killed by this spell rises at the start of your next turn as a zombie permanently under your command.

## Fire Storm
*7th-level evocation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 150 feet | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Instantaneous
A storm made up of sheets of roaring flame appears. The area of the storm consists of up to ten 10-foot cubes, which you can arrange as you wish. Each cube must have at least one face adjacent to the face of another cube. Each creature in the area must make a DEX save: **7d10 fire** on failure, half on success. The fire ignites flammable objects not being worn or carried. If you wish, plant life in the area is unaffected.

## Forcecage
*7th-level evocation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 100 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (ruby dust worth 1,500 gp) | **Duration:** 1 hour
An immobile, invisible, cube-shaped prison of magical force springs into existence around an area you choose. The prison can be a cage (up to 20-foot cube, bars spaced 1/2 inch apart) or a box (up to 10-foot cube, solid wall of force, blocking passage and even teleportation). A creature inside can't leave by nonmagical means. If the creature tries to use teleportation or interplanar travel, it must first make a CHA save. On failure, the attempt fails. This spell can't be dispelled by Dispel Magic.

## Magnificent Mansion
*7th-level conjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 1 minute | **Range:** 300 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (a miniature portal carved from ivory, a small piece of polished marble, and a tiny silver spoon, each worth at least 5 gp) | **Duration:** 24 hours
You conjure an extradimensional dwelling. The entrance is 5 feet wide and 10 feet tall. You and any creature you designate can enter. The dwelling consists of a foyer and up to 100 rooms. The atmosphere is clean, fresh, and warm. Furnishings and decor are as you choose. A staff of 100 near-transparent servants attend all who enter. The food provided is sufficient to serve a nine-course banquet for up to 100 people. Creatures and objects inside are ejected when the spell ends.

## Mirage Arcane
*7th-level illusion*
- **Casting Time:** 10 minutes | **Range:** Sight | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** 10 days
You make terrain in an area up to one mile square look, sound, smell, and even feel like some other sort of terrain. The terrain's general shape remains the same, however. Open fields or a road can be made to look like a swamp, hill, crevasse, or some other terrain. Structures can be added (or existing ones altered to appear differently). The illusion includes audible, visual, tactile, and olfactory elements. Creatures with truesight can see through the illusion.

## Plane Shift
*7th-level conjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** Touch | **Components:** V, S, M (a forked, metal rod worth at least 250 gp, attuned to a particular plane of existence) | **Duration:** Instantaneous
You and up to eight willing creatures who link hands are transported to a different plane of existence. You can specify a target destination in general terms and appear in or near that destination. Alternatively, you can use this spell as an attack: make a melee spell attack against an unwilling creature. On hit, the creature must make a CHA save. On failure, the creature is transported to a random location on the plane you specify.

## Prismatic Spray
*7th-level evocation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** Self (60-foot cone) | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Instantaneous
Eight multicolored rays of light flash from your hand. Each creature in a 60-foot cone must make a DEX save. For each target, roll a d8 to determine the ray: **1 Red**: 10d6 fire (half on save). **2 Orange**: 10d6 acid (half). **3 Yellow**: 10d6 lightning (half). **4 Green**: 10d6 poison (half). **5 Blue**: 10d6 cold (half). **6 Indigo**: CON save or restrained, then CON save each turn — 3 failures = petrified, 3 successes = free. **7 Violet**: WIS save or blinded, then WIS save at start of next turn — failure = transported to another plane, success = no longer blinded. **8**: Re-roll twice, ignoring 8s.

## Project Image
*7th-level illusion*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 500 miles | **Components:** V, S, M (a small replica of you made from materials worth at least 5 gp) | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 day
You create an illusory copy of yourself that lasts for the duration. The copy can appear at any location within range that you have seen before, regardless of intervening obstacles. You can use your action to move this image and cause it to gesture, speak, and behave however you choose. It mimics your mannerisms perfectly. You can see through its eyes and hear through its ears as if you were in its space. As a bonus action, you can switch between your own senses and the image's. Physical interaction reveals it as an illusion.

## Regenerate
*7th-level transmutation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 minute | **Range:** Touch | **Components:** V, S, M (a prayer wheel and holy water) | **Duration:** 1 hour
You touch a creature and stimulate its natural healing ability. The target regains **4d8 + 15 hit points**. For the duration of the spell, the target regains **1 hit point at the start of each of its turns** (10 HP per minute). The target's severed body members (fingers, legs, tails, and so on), if any, are restored after 2 minutes.

## Resurrection
*7th-level necromancy*
- **Casting Time:** 1 hour | **Range:** Touch | **Components:** V, S, M (a diamond worth at least 1,000 gp, consumed) | **Duration:** Instantaneous
You touch a dead creature that has been dead for no more than a century, that didn't die of old age, and that isn't undead. The target returns to life with **all its hit points**. This spell neutralizes any poisons and cures normal diseases. It doesn't remove magical diseases, curses, or similar effects. The target takes a **-4 penalty to all d20 rolls**, reduced by 1 each long rest.

## Reverse Gravity
*7th-level transmutation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 100 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (a lodestone and iron filings) | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
This spell reverses gravity in a 50-foot-radius, 100-foot high cylinder centered on a point within range. All creatures and objects that aren't somehow anchored to the ground in the area fall upward to the top of the area. A creature can make a DEX save to grab a fixed object it can reach, avoiding the fall. If some solid object (like a ceiling) is encountered during the fall, falling objects and creatures strike it as in a normal downward fall. When the spell ends, affected objects and creatures fall back down.

## Sequester
*7th-level transmutation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** Touch | **Components:** V, S, M (a powder composed of diamond, emerald, ruby, and sapphire dust worth at least 5,000 gp, consumed) | **Duration:** Until dispelled
By means of this spell, a willing creature or an object can be hidden away, safe from detection for the duration. The target becomes invisible and can't be targeted by divination spells or perceived through scrying sensors. If the target is a creature, it falls into a state of suspended animation. Time ceases to flow for it, and it doesn't age. You can set a condition for the spell to end early.

## Simulacrum
*7th-level illusion*
- **Casting Time:** 12 hours | **Range:** Touch | **Components:** V, S, M (snow or ice in quantities sufficient to make a life-size copy; hair, fingernail, or other piece of that creature's body placed inside the snow or ice; and powdered ruby worth 1,500 gp, consumed) | **Duration:** Until dispelled
You shape an illusory duplicate of one beast or humanoid that is within range for the entire casting. The duplicate is a creature, partially real, formed from ice or snow. It has half the hit point maximum of the original creature. It obeys your commands. It can't learn or become more powerful. It lacks the ability to regain expended spell slots. If the simulacrum is damaged, you can repair it in an alchemical laboratory using components worth 100 gp per HP restored. If it drops to 0 HP, it reverts to snow and is destroyed. If you cast this spell again, any currently active duplicate is instantly destroyed.

## Symbol
*7th-level abjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 1 minute | **Range:** Touch | **Components:** V, S, M (mercury, phosphorus, and powdered diamond and opal worth at least 1,000 gp total, consumed) | **Duration:** Until dispelled or triggered
You inscribe a harmful glyph on a surface (object, door, floor, wall, etc.) or within an object (book, scroll, etc.). The glyph is nearly invisible (Investigation check vs. your spell save DC). When triggered (creature moves within 60 feet of the glyph), choose one effect: **Death** (10d10 necrotic, CON save half), **Discord** (argue for 1 minute), **Fear** (frightened, flee for 1 minute), **Hopelessness** (CHA save or overwhelmed with despair for 1 minute), **Insanity** (INT save or go insane for 1 minute — can't take actions, babbles), **Pain** (incapacitated, CON save), **Sleep** (unconscious, WIS save), **Stunning** (stunned, WIS save). Each creature in 60-foot radius is affected.

## Teleport
*7th-level conjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 10 feet | **Components:** V | **Duration:** Instantaneous
This spell instantly transports you and up to eight willing creatures within range to a destination you select. The destination must be known to you, and it must be on the same plane. Your familiarity with the destination determines the chance of arriving on target: **Permanent Circle** (auto-success), **Associated Object** (auto), **Very Familiar** (01-97 on target, 98-99 off target, 00 mishap), **Seen Casually** (01-88 on target, 89-94 off target, 95-98 similar area, 99-00 mishap), **Viewed Once** (01-76 on target, 77-88 off, 89-96 similar, 97-00 mishap), **Description** (01-54 on target, 55-73 off, 74-92 similar, 93-00 mishap), **False Destination** (mishap only). Mishap: each creature takes 3d10 force and the DM rerolls on the table.
