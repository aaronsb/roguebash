# 5th-Level Spells

## Animate Objects
*5th-level transmutation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 120 feet | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
Objects animate at your command. Choose up to ten nonmagical objects not being worn or carried. Medium counts as 2, Large as 4, Huge as 8. Each animated object has stats based on its size (Tiny: HP 20, AC 18, ATK +8, 1d4+4; Small: HP 25, AC 16, ATK +6, 1d8+2; Medium: HP 40, AC 13, ATK +5, 2d6+1; Large: HP 50, AC 10, ATK +6, 2d10+2; Huge: HP 80, AC 10, ATK +8, 2d12+4). As a bonus action, you command any or all of them. They act on your initiative. **At Higher Levels.** +2 objects per slot above 5th.

## Antilife Shell
*5th-level abjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** Self (10-foot radius) | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour
A shimmering barrier extends out from you in a 10-foot radius and moves with you, remaining centered on you. The barrier prevents creatures other than undead and constructs from passing or reaching through. An affected creature can cast spells or make attacks with ranged or reach weapons through the barrier. If you move so that an affected creature is forced through the barrier, the spell ends.

## Arcane Hand
*5th-level evocation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 120 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (an eggshell and a snakeskin glove) | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
You create a Large hand of shimmering, translucent force (AC 20, HP = your HP max, STR 26 (+8), DEX 10 (+0)). The hand moves at your command (bonus action, 60 feet). Actions: **Clenched Fist** (melee spell attack, 4d8 force), **Forceful Hand** (push target 5 ft. + 5 x spellcasting mod, STR contest to resist), **Grasping Hand** (grapple, STR contest, 2d6+spellcasting mod bludgeoning while grappled), **Interposing Hand** (half cover between you and a target, target can't move through). **At Higher Levels.** Clenched Fist +2d8, Grasping Hand +2d6 per slot above 5th.

## Awaken
*5th-level transmutation*
- **Casting Time:** 8 hours | **Range:** Touch | **Components:** V, S, M (an agate worth at least 1,000 gp, consumed) | **Duration:** Instantaneous
You touch a Huge or smaller beast or plant (beast must have INT 3 or less). The target gains an Intelligence of 10 and the ability to speak one language you know. If it is a plant, it gains the ability to move its limbs, roots, vines, etc. freely. The awakened creature is charmed by you for 30 days or until you or your companions harm it. When the charm ends, the creature chooses its attitude based on how you treated it.

## Cloudkill
*5th-level conjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 120 feet | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes
You create a 20-foot-radius sphere of poisonous, yellow-green fog centered on a point you choose. The fog spreads around corners, heavily obscures, and moves 10 feet away from you at the start of each of your turns. A creature that starts its turn in the fog or enters it for the first time must make a CON save: **5d8 poison** on failure, half on success. The fog moves along the ground and sinks into openings. **At Higher Levels.** +1d8 per slot above 5th.

## Commune
*5th-level divination (ritual)*
- **Casting Time:** 1 minute | **Range:** Self | **Components:** V, S, M (incense and a vial of holy or unholy water) | **Duration:** 1 minute
You contact your deity or a divine proxy and ask up to three questions that can be answered with "yes," "no," or "unclear." Divine beings aren't necessarily omniscient, so you might receive "unclear" for questions about the future, beyond the deity's knowledge, or that could be misleading. Casting more than once before a long rest: 25% cumulative chance of no answer per additional casting.

## Commune with Nature
*5th-level divination (ritual)*
- **Casting Time:** 1 minute | **Range:** Self | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Instantaneous
You briefly become one with nature and gain knowledge of the surrounding territory (outdoors: 3-mile radius; underground: 300-foot radius). You can learn up to three facts about the area: terrain and bodies of water, prevalent plants/minerals/animals/peoples, powerful celestials/fey/fiends/elementals/undead, influence from other planes, or buildings.

## Cone of Cold
*5th-level evocation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** Self (60-foot cone) | **Components:** V, S, M (a small crystal or glass cone) | **Duration:** Instantaneous
A blast of cold air erupts from your hands. Each creature in a 60-foot cone must make a CON save: **8d8 cold** on failure, half on success. A creature killed by this spell becomes a frozen statue until it thaws. **At Higher Levels.** +1d8 per slot above 5th.

## Conjure Elemental
*5th-level conjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 1 minute | **Range:** 90 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (burning incense for air, soft clay for earth, sulfur and phosphorus for fire, or water and sand for water) | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour
You call forth an elemental servant. Choose an area of air, earth, fire, or water that fills a 10-foot cube within range. An elemental of CR 5 or lower appropriate to the area appears. It is friendly and obeys your verbal commands. If your concentration is broken, the elemental doesn't disappear — it becomes hostile and might attack. It can't be dismissed by you. It disappears when it drops to 0 HP or the spell ends. **At Higher Levels.** +1 CR per slot above 5th.

## Contact Other Plane
*5th-level divination (ritual)*
- **Casting Time:** 1 minute | **Range:** Self | **Components:** V | **Duration:** 1 minute
You mentally contact a demigod, the spirit of a long-dead sage, or some other mysterious entity from another plane. Contacting this extraplanar intelligence can strain your mind: make a DC 15 INT save. On failure, you take 6d6 psychic damage and are insane (can't take actions, understand language, or communicate) until you finish a long rest (Greater Restoration cures this). On success, you can ask the entity up to five questions, each answered with a one-word answer (yes, no, maybe, never, irrelevant, unclear).

## Contagion
*5th-level necromancy*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** Touch | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** 7 days
Your touch inflicts disease. Make a melee spell attack. On hit, the target is poisoned. At the end of each of the poisoned target's turns, it must make a CON save. After three failed saves, the target is no longer poisoned but instead takes on a disease you choose (Blinding Sickness, Filth Fever, Flesh Rot, Mindfire, Seizure, or Slimy Doom — each with specific effects). After three successes, the target recovers and the spell ends. A Lesser Restoration or similar magic can end the disease.

## Creation
*5th-level illusion*
- **Casting Time:** 1 minute | **Range:** 30 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (a tiny piece of matter of the same type of the item you plan to create) | **Duration:** Special
You pull wisps of shadow material from the Shadowfell to create a nonliving object of vegetable matter within range: soft goods, rope, wood, or something similar. Duration by material: Vegetable (1 day), Stone/crystal (12 hours), Precious metals (1 hour), Gems (10 minutes), Adamantine/mithral (1 minute). The object can be no larger than a 5-foot cube. **At Higher Levels.** Cube size increases by 5 feet per slot above 5th.

## Dispel Evil and Good
*5th-level abjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** Self | **Components:** V, S, M (holy water or powdered silver and iron) | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
Shimmering energy surrounds you. You have advantage on attack rolls and saves against aberrations, celestials, elementals, fey, fiends, and undead. You can end the spell early for one of these effects: **Break Enchantment** (touch, end one charm/frighten/possess on a creature), **Dismissal** (melee spell attack vs aberration/celestial/elemental/fey/fiend, on hit CHA save or sent to home plane).

## Dominate Person
*5th-level enchantment*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 60 feet | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
You attempt to beguile a humanoid. WIS save (advantage if you or your companions are fighting it). On failure, charmed and you establish telepathic link. You can command it (no action) or take total control (your action). Each time it takes damage, new WIS save. **At Higher Levels.** 6th: up to 10 min. 7th: up to 1 hour. 8th+: up to 8 hours.

## Dream
*5th-level illusion*
- **Casting Time:** 1 minute | **Range:** Special | **Components:** V, S, M (a handful of sand, a dab of ink, and a writing quill plucked from a sleeping bird) | **Duration:** 8 hours
You shape a creature's dreams. Choose a creature known to you on the same plane. A messenger (you or a willing creature you touch) enters the target's dreams and can converse with the dreaming target. The messenger can also make the dream a nightmare: the target must make a WIS save or take 3d6 psychic damage and gains no benefit from the rest. A target that isn't asleep when you cast isn't affected but becomes a target if it falls asleep during the duration.

## Flame Strike
*5th-level evocation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 60 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (pinch of sulfur) | **Duration:** Instantaneous
A vertical column of divine fire roars down in a 10-foot-radius, 40-foot-high cylinder centered on a point within range. Each creature must make a DEX save: **4d6 fire + 4d6 radiant** on failure, half on success. **At Higher Levels.** +1d6 fire per slot above 5th.

## Geas
*5th-level enchantment*
- **Casting Time:** 1 minute | **Range:** 60 feet | **Components:** V | **Duration:** 30 days
You place a magical command on a creature (WIS save, charmed creatures and creatures that can't be charmed are immune/auto-succeed). The creature must follow a course of activity or refrain from one, as you decide. If the creature disobeys, it takes 5d10 psychic damage (once per day max). Remove Curse, Greater Restoration, or Wish can end it. **At Higher Levels.** 7th: 1 year. 9th: until dispelled.

## Greater Restoration
*5th-level abjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** Touch | **Components:** V, S, M (diamond dust worth at least 100 gp, consumed) | **Duration:** Instantaneous
You imbue a creature you touch with positive energy to undo a debilitating effect. You can reduce the target's exhaustion level by one, or end one of the following effects: charmed/petrified, a curse (including attunement to a cursed magic item), any reduction to an ability score, or one effect reducing the target's HP maximum.

## Hallow
*5th-level evocation*
- **Casting Time:** 24 hours | **Range:** Touch | **Components:** V, S, M (herbs, oils, and incense worth at least 1,000 gp, consumed) | **Duration:** Until dispelled
You touch a point and infuse an area around it (up to 60-foot radius) with holy (or unholy) power. Celestials, elementals, fey, fiends, and undead can't enter the area, and creatures in the area can't be charmed, frightened, or possessed by such creatures. You also bind an extra effect to the area (choose one): Courage, Darkness, Daylight, Energy Protection, Energy Vulnerability, Everlasting Rest, Extradimensional Interference, Fear, Silence, or Tongues.

## Hold Monster
*5th-level enchantment*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 90 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (a small, straight piece of iron) | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
Choose a creature that you can see. The target must succeed on a WIS save or be **paralyzed** for the duration. This spell has no creature type restriction (unlike Hold Person). At the end of each of its turns, the target can make another WIS save, ending the effect on a success. **At Higher Levels.** One additional creature per slot above 5th.

## Insect Plague
*5th-level conjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 300 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (a few grains of sugar, some kernels of grain, and a smear of fat) | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes
Swarming, biting locusts fill a 20-foot-radius sphere centered on a point you choose. The sphere spreads around corners. The area is difficult terrain and lightly obscured. A creature entering or starting its turn there must make a CON save: **4d10 piercing** on failure, half on success. **At Higher Levels.** +1d10 per slot above 5th.

## Legend Lore
*5th-level divination*
- **Casting Time:** 10 minutes | **Range:** Self | **Components:** V, S, M (incense worth at least 250 gp, consumed, and four ivory strips worth at least 50 gp each) | **Duration:** Instantaneous
Name or describe a person, place, or object. The spell brings to your mind a brief summary of the significant lore about the thing you named. The lore might consist of current tales, forgotten stories, or even secret lore. The more information you already have, the more precise and detailed the information you receive.

## Mass Cure Wounds
*5th-level evocation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 60 feet | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Instantaneous
A wave of healing energy washes out from a point of your choice within range. Choose up to six creatures in a 30-foot-radius sphere. Each target regains hit points equal to **3d8 + your spellcasting ability modifier**. No effect on undead or constructs. **At Higher Levels.** +1d8 per slot above 5th.

## Mislead
*5th-level illusion*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** Self | **Components:** S | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour
You become invisible at the same time that an illusory double of you appears where you are standing. The double lasts for the duration but the invisibility ends if you attack or cast a spell. You can use your action to move the double up to twice your speed and make it gesture, speak, and behave in whatever way you choose.

## Modify Memory
*5th-level enchantment*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 30 feet | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
You attempt to reshape another creature's memories. The creature must make a WIS save. If charmed by you or your companion, it makes this save with disadvantage. On failure, the target becomes charmed and incapacitated, appearing lost in thought. You can modify a memory of an event within the last 24 hours that lasted no more than 10 minutes. You can eliminate all memory of it, allow the target to recall it clearly, change details, or create a memory of some other event. **At Higher Levels.** 6th: 7 days ago. 7th: 30 days. 8th: 1 year. 9th: any time.

## Passwall
*5th-level transmutation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 30 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of sesame seeds) | **Duration:** 1 hour
A passage appears at a point of your choice on a wooden, plaster, or stone surface (up to 5 feet wide, 8 feet tall, and 20 feet deep). The passage creates no instability in surrounding structures. When the opening disappears, any creatures or objects inside are safely ejected to unoccupied spaces nearest to where you cast the spell.

## Planar Binding
*5th-level abjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 1 hour | **Range:** 60 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (a jewel worth at least 1,000 gp, consumed) | **Duration:** 24 hours
You bind a celestial, elemental, fey, or fiend that must be within range for the entire casting (typically trapped via Magic Circle). The target must make a CHA save. On failure, it must serve you for the duration. If the creature was summoned or created by another spell, that spell's duration is extended to match this spell's duration. **At Higher Levels.** 6th: 10 days. 7th: 30 days. 8th: 180 days. 9th: a year and a day.

## Raise Dead
*5th-level necromancy*
- **Casting Time:** 1 hour | **Range:** Touch | **Components:** V, S, M (a diamond worth at least 500 gp, consumed) | **Duration:** Instantaneous
You return a dead creature you touch to life, provided that it has been dead no longer than 10 days. The creature returns to life with **1 hit point**. This spell also neutralizes any poisons and cures nonmagical diseases that affected the creature at the time it died. This spell doesn't remove magical diseases, curses, or similar effects; these must be dealt with before the spell is cast. This spell can't return an undead creature to life. The creature takes a **-4 penalty to all attack rolls, saving throws, and ability checks**. The penalty is reduced by 1 every time the target finishes a long rest, until it disappears. Coming back from the dead is an ordeal.

## Reincarnate
*5th-level transmutation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 hour | **Range:** Touch | **Components:** V, S, M (rare oils and unguents worth at least 1,000 gp, consumed) | **Duration:** Instantaneous
You touch a dead humanoid or a piece of a dead humanoid. Provided that the creature has been dead no longer than 10 days, the spell forms a new adult body for it and then calls the soul to enter that body. The DM rolls a d100 to determine the new race (table provided). The reincarnated creature recalls its former life and retains the capabilities it had in its original form, except it exchanges its original race for the new one.

## Scrying
*5th-level divination*
- **Casting Time:** 10 minutes | **Range:** Self | **Components:** V, S, M (a focus worth at least 1,000 gp — a crystal ball, silver mirror, or font of holy water) | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes
You can see and hear a particular creature. The target must make a WIS save (modified by familiarity and connections: secondhand knowledge +5, firsthand +0, familiar -5; likeness/possession -2 each, body part -4). On failure, you can see the target and immediate surroundings. You can cast spells through the sensor that target only the creature.

## Seeming
*5th-level illusion*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 30 feet | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** 8 hours
This spell allows you to change the appearance of any number of creatures that you can see within range. Each target appears different. You can make them seem shorter, fatter, thinner, change clothing and equipment appearance, etc. The changes fail to hold up to physical inspection. A creature can use its action to inspect with an Investigation check against your spell save DC. An unwilling target can make a CHA save to resist.

## Telekinesis
*5th-level transmutation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 60 feet | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes
You gain the ability to move or manipulate creatures or objects by thought. You can use your action each round for one of these: **Creature** (STR contest, on success move creature up to 30 feet, restrained in your grip for the round; Huge or larger creature has advantage), **Object** (move an object up to 1,000 pounds up to 30 feet; can exert fine control on objects, like manipulating a tool or opening a door).

## Telepathic Bond
*5th-level divination (ritual)*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 30 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (pieces of eggshell from two different kinds of creatures) | **Duration:** 1 hour
You forge a telepathic link among up to eight willing creatures of your choice within range. Each target can communicate telepathically with any other target. The communication is possible over any distance, though it can't extend to other planes of existence.

## Teleportation Circle
*5th-level conjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 1 minute | **Range:** 10 feet | **Components:** V, M (rare chalks and inks infused with precious gems worth 50 gp, consumed) | **Duration:** 1 round
You draw a 10-foot-diameter circle on the ground inscribed with sigils that link your location to a permanent teleportation circle of your choice whose sigil sequence you know. A shimmering portal opens within the circle you drew and remains open until the end of your next turn. Any creature that enters the portal instantly appears within 5 feet of the destination circle (or the nearest unoccupied space). You can create a permanent teleportation circle by casting this spell in the same location every day for one year.

## Tree Stride
*5th-level conjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** Self | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
You gain the ability to enter a tree and move from inside it to inside another tree of the same kind within 500 feet. Both trees must be living and at least the same size as you. You use 5 feet of movement to step into the first tree. You instantly know the location of all other trees of the same kind within 500 feet and can move to one (using 5 feet of movement to exit). You can use this feature once per round.

## Wall of Force
*5th-level evocation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 120 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of powder made by crushing a clear gemstone) | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes
An invisible wall of force springs into existence at a point you choose. The wall appears in any orientation you choose. It can be flat or hemispherical. Up to ten 10-foot-by-10-foot panels. Each panel must be contiguous with another panel. Nothing can physically pass through the wall. It extends into the Ethereal Plane, blocking ethereal travel. The wall is immune to all damage and can't be dispelled by Dispel Magic. A Disintegrate spell destroys the wall instantly.

## Wall of Stone
*5th-level evocation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 120 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (a small block of granite) | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes
A nonmagical wall of solid stone springs into existence. Up to ten 10-foot-by-10-foot panels, each 6 inches thick. Each panel must be contiguous with another panel. If the wall cuts through a creature's space, the creature is pushed to one side (creature's choice). If a creature would be surrounded on all sides, it can make a DEX save to move up to its speed to escape. The wall can be destroyed (each panel: AC 15, HP 30 per inch of thickness, vulnerability to thunder damage). If you maintain concentration for the full duration, the wall becomes permanent and can't be dispelled.
