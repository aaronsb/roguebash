# 6th-Level Spells

## Blade Barrier
*6th-level evocation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 90 feet | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes
You create a vertical wall of whirling, razor-sharp blades made of magical energy. The wall appears within range and lasts for the duration. You can make a straight wall up to 100 feet long, 20 feet high, and 5 feet thick, or a ringed wall up to 60 feet in diameter, 20 feet high, and 5 feet thick. The wall provides three-quarters cover and its area is difficult terrain. A creature entering or starting its turn there makes a DEX save: **6d10 slashing** on failure, half on success.

## Chain Lightning
*6th-level evocation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 150 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (a bit of fur, a piece of amber, glass, or a crystal rod, and three silver pins) | **Duration:** Instantaneous
You create a bolt of lightning that arcs toward a target. Three bolts then leap from that target to as many as three other targets within 30 feet of the first. Each target must make a DEX save: **10d8 lightning** on failure, half on success. **At Higher Levels.** +1 additional bolt per slot above 6th.

## Circle of Death
*6th-level necromancy*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 150 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (the powder of a crushed black pearl worth at least 500 gp) | **Duration:** Instantaneous
A sphere of negative energy ripples out in a 60-foot-radius sphere from a point within range. Each creature in that area must make a CON save: **8d6 necrotic** on failure, half on success. **At Higher Levels.** +2d6 per slot above 6th.

## Conjure Fey
*6th-level conjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 1 minute | **Range:** 90 feet | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour
You summon a fey creature of CR 6 or lower, or a fey spirit that takes the form of a beast of CR 6 or lower. It is friendly and obeys your verbal commands. If your concentration is broken, the fey becomes hostile. **At Higher Levels.** +1 CR per slot above 6th.

## Contingency
*6th-level evocation*
- **Casting Time:** 10 minutes | **Range:** Self | **Components:** V, S, M (a statuette of yourself carved from ivory and decorated with gems worth at least 1,500 gp) | **Duration:** 10 days
Choose a spell of 5th level or lower that you can cast, that has a casting time of 1 action, and that can target you. You cast that spell — called the contingent spell — as part of casting Contingency, expending spell slots for both. The contingent spell doesn't come into effect immediately but takes effect when a certain circumstance occurs (which you describe when casting). You can have only one contingency active at a time.

## Create Undead
*6th-level necromancy*
- **Casting Time:** 1 minute | **Range:** 10 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (one clay pot filled with grave dirt, one clay pot filled with brackish water, and one 150 gp black onyx stone for each corpse) | **Duration:** Instantaneous
You create up to three ghouls under your command. You can issue mental commands (bonus action). Creatures remain under your control for 24 hours; recast to maintain. **At Higher Levels.** 7th: 4 ghouls or 2 ghasts/wights. 8th: 5 ghouls or 3 ghasts/wights or 2 mummies. 9th: 6 ghouls or 4 ghasts/wights or 3 mummies.

## Disintegrate
*6th-level transmutation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 60 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (a lodestone and a pinch of dust) | **Duration:** Instantaneous
A thin green ray springs from your pointing finger to a target. The target can be a creature, an object, or a creation of magical force, such as the wall created by Wall of Force. A creature targeted must make a DEX save: **10d6+40 force** on failure. **The target is disintegrated if this damage reduces it to 0 HP.** A disintegrated creature and everything it's wearing and carrying (except magic items) are reduced to a pile of fine gray dust. The creature can be restored only by True Resurrection or Wish. This spell automatically disintegrates a Large or smaller nonmagical object or a creation of magical force. **At Higher Levels.** +3d6 per slot above 6th.

## Eyebite
*6th-level necromancy*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** Self | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
For the spell's duration, your eyes become an inky void imbued with dread power. One creature of your choice within 60 feet must succeed on a WIS save or be affected by one effect you choose: **Asleep** (falls unconscious, wakes if it takes damage or someone uses action to wake it), **Panicked** (frightened, must Dash away from you), or **Sickened** (disadvantage on attacks and ability checks). Each turn you can use your action to target a new creature (can't target a creature that has succeeded on a save already).

## Find the Path
*6th-level divination*
- **Casting Time:** 1 minute | **Range:** Self | **Components:** V, S, M (a set of divinatory tools worth 100 gp and an object from the location you wish to find) | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 day
This spell allows you to find the shortest, most direct physical route to a specific fixed location that you are familiar with on the same plane of existence. You know the distance and direction to the destination. You always know the most direct route to the destination.

## Flesh to Stone
*6th-level transmutation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 60 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of lime, water, and earth) | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
You attempt to turn one creature that you can see to stone. The target makes a CON save. On failure, the creature is restrained as its flesh begins to harden. The restrained creature must make another CON save at the end of each of its turns. Three successes: spell ends. Three failures: **turned to stone and petrified permanently** (until freed by Greater Restoration or similar). If concentration is broken before full petrification, the effect ends.

## Forbiddance
*6th-level abjuration (ritual)*
- **Casting Time:** 10 minutes | **Range:** Touch | **Components:** V, S, M (a sprinkling of holy water, rare incense, and powdered ruby worth at least 1,000 gp) | **Duration:** 1 day
You create a ward against magical travel within a 40,000-square-foot area up to 30 feet high. For the duration, no creatures can teleport into the area or use portals. Creatures that first enter the area or start their turn there can also be damaged: choose one type (celestials, elementals, fey, fiends, undead); creatures of that type take 5d10 radiant or necrotic damage. Casting daily for 30 days makes it permanent.

## Freezing Sphere
*6th-level evocation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 300 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (a small crystal sphere) | **Duration:** Instantaneous
A frigid globe of cold energy streaks from your fingertips to a point of your choice within range, where it explodes in a 60-foot-radius sphere. Each creature must make a CON save: **10d6 cold** on failure, half on success. If the globe strikes a body of water, it freezes the water to a depth of 6 inches over 30-foot-square area. You can also refrain from firing the globe after creating it and instead hold it (it is cold but doesn't harm you). **At Higher Levels.** +1d6 per slot above 6th.

## Globe of Invulnerability
*6th-level abjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** Self (10-foot radius) | **Components:** V, S, M (a glass or crystal bead that shatters when the spell ends) | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
An immobile, faintly shimmering barrier springs into existence in a 10-foot radius around you. Any spell of 5th level or lower cast from outside the barrier can't affect creatures or objects within it, even if the spell is cast using a higher level slot. Such a spell can target creatures and objects within the barrier, but the spell has no effect on them. Similarly, the area within the barrier is excluded from areas affected by such spells. **At Higher Levels.** Blocks one additional spell level per slot above 6th.

## Guards and Wards
*6th-level abjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 10 minutes | **Range:** Touch | **Components:** V, S, M (burning incense, brimstone, oil, knotted string, and umber hulk blood, plus a small silver rod worth at least 10 gp) | **Duration:** 24 hours
You create a ward that protects up to 2,500 square feet of floor space. Effects include: corridors filled with fog, locked and arcane-locked doors, stairs filled with webs, stinking cloud at intersections, and a suggestion effect at certain locations. Casting daily for a year makes it permanent.

## Harm
*6th-level necromancy*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 60 feet | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Instantaneous
You unleash a virulent disease on a creature. The target must make a CON save: **14d6 necrotic** on failure (half on success). The target's HP maximum is reduced by the same amount for 1 hour. The target dies if its HP maximum is reduced to 0 by this spell. Has no effect on undead or constructs.

## Heal
*6th-level evocation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 60 feet | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Instantaneous
Choose a creature that you can see within range. A surge of positive energy washes through the creature, causing it to regain **70 hit points**. This spell also ends blindness, deafness, and any diseases affecting the target. No effect on undead or constructs. **At Higher Levels.** +10 HP per slot above 6th.

## Heroes' Feast
*6th-level conjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 10 minutes | **Range:** 30 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (a gem-encrusted bowl worth at least 1,000 gp, consumed) | **Duration:** Instantaneous
You bring forth a great feast. Up to twelve creatures can partake of the feast (1 hour to consume). The feast disappears at the end of the hour. Creatures that partake gain several benefits for 24 hours: cured of all diseases and poison, immune to poison and being frightened, advantage on WIS saves, HP maximum increases by 2d10, and they gain that many extra hit points.

## Instant Summons
*6th-level conjuration (ritual)*
- **Casting Time:** 1 minute | **Range:** Touch | **Components:** V, S, M (a sapphire worth 1,000 gp) | **Duration:** Until dispelled
You touch an object weighing 10 pounds or less that is at most 6 feet in any dimension and link it to the sapphire gem. Thereafter, you can crush the sapphire and speak the item's name to have it appear in your hand. The item can be on another plane of existence. If another creature is holding the item, crushing the gem doesn't transport it, but instead you learn who possesses the object and roughly where they are.

## Irresistible Dance
*6th-level enchantment*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 30 feet | **Components:** V | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
Choose one creature that you can see. The target begins a comic dance in place: shuffling, tapping its feet, and capering. The target must use all its movement to dance without leaving its space and has **disadvantage on DEX saves and attack rolls**. Other creatures have **advantage on attack rolls** against it. As an action, the dancing creature makes a WIS save; on success, the spell ends on it.

## Magic Jar
*6th-level necromancy*
- **Casting Time:** 1 minute | **Range:** Self | **Components:** V, S, M (a gem, crystal, reliquary, or some other ornamental container worth at least 500 gp) | **Duration:** Until dispelled
Your body falls into a catatonic state as your soul leaves it and enters the container. While your soul inhabits the container, you can attempt to possess any humanoid within 100 feet of the container. The target must make a CHA save. On failure, your soul moves into the target's body, and the target's soul becomes trapped in the container. You retain your alignment, INT, WIS, CHA, and class features; otherwise you gain the target's statistics.

## Mass Suggestion
*6th-level enchantment*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 60 feet | **Components:** V, M (a snake's tongue and either a bit of honeycomb or a drop of sweet oil) | **Duration:** 24 hours
You suggest a course of activity to up to twelve creatures of your choice that you can see within range and that can hear and understand you. Each that fails a WIS save pursues the course of action you described. The suggestion must be worded to sound reasonable. **At Higher Levels.** 7th: 10 days. 8th: 30 days. 9th: a year and a day.

## Move Earth
*6th-level transmutation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 120 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (an iron blade and a small bag containing a mixture of soils — clay, loam, and sand) | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 2 hours
Choose an area of terrain no larger than 40 feet on a side. You can reshape dirt, sand, or clay in the area in any manner you choose for the duration. You can raise or lower the area's elevation, create or fill in a trench, erect or flatten a wall, or form a pillar. The extent of the change can't exceed half the area's largest dimension. Changes occur over 10 minutes. This spell can't manipulate natural stone or stone construction.

## Planar Ally
*6th-level conjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 10 minutes | **Range:** 60 feet | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Instantaneous
You beseech an otherworldly entity for aid. The being you chose must be known to you: a god, primordial, demon prince, or some other cosmic power. That entity sends a celestial, elemental, or fiend loyal to it to aid you. The creature is under no compulsion to behave in any particular way. You must negotiate with it (payment for services: 100 gp per minute of help, 1,000 gp per hour, 10,000 gp per day or hazardous task, or a quest).

## Programmed Illusion
*6th-level illusion*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 120 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (a bit of fleece and jade dust worth at least 25 gp) | **Duration:** Until dispelled
You create an illusion of an object, creature, or other visible phenomenon within range that activates when a specific condition occurs. Until then, the illusion is imperceptible. It can be no larger than a 30-foot cube and lasts for 5 minutes once triggered. It seems completely real, including sounds, smells, and temperature. You decide the illusion's behavior, including sounds, movement, and actions.

## Sunbeam
*6th-level evocation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** Self (60-foot line) | **Components:** V, S, M (a magnifying glass) | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
A beam of brilliant light flashes out from your hand in a 5-foot-wide, 60-foot-long line. Each creature in the line must make a CON save: **6d8 radiant** on failure (half on success), and is **blinded until your next turn** on failure. Undead and oozes have disadvantage on the save. You can create a new line of radiance as your action on any turn until the spell ends. For the duration, a mote of brilliant radiance shines in your hand, shedding bright light 30 feet and dim light 30 feet beyond.

## Transport via Plants
*6th-level conjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 10 feet | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** 1 round
This spell creates a magical link between a Large or larger inanimate plant within range and another plant, at any distance, on the same plane of existence. You must have seen or touched the destination plant at least once before. For the duration, any creature can step into the target plant and exit from the destination plant by using 5 feet of movement.

## True Seeing
*6th-level divination*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** Touch | **Components:** V, S, M (an ointment for the eyes that costs 25 gp, made from mushroom powder, saffron, and fat, consumed) | **Duration:** 1 hour
This spell gives the willing creature you touch the ability to see things as they actually are. For the duration, the creature has **truesight** (120 feet), notices secret doors hidden by magic, and can see into the Ethereal Plane.

## Wall of Ice
*6th-level evocation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 120 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (a small piece of quartz) | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes
You create a wall of ice on a solid surface. You can form it into a hemisphere or dome (up to 10-foot radius), or ten 10-foot panels (each 10x10x1 feet). Creatures in the wall's area when it appears must make a DEX save: **10d6 cold** on failure, half on success (and pushed to one side). The wall has AC 12, 30 HP per 10-foot section, and is vulnerable to fire damage. Reducing a section to 0 HP leaves behind a sheet of frigid air (creature entering or ending turn there: CON save, 5d6 cold on failure, half on success). **At Higher Levels.** +2d6 initial and +1d6 for the cold air per slot above 6th.

## Wall of Thorns
*6th-level conjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 120 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (a handful of thorns) | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes
You create a wall of tough, pliable, tangled brush bristling with needle-sharp thorns. The wall appears within range on a solid surface. Up to 60 feet long, 10 feet high, 5 feet thick (or less). The wall blocks line of sight. When a creature enters or starts its turn in the wall: DEX save, **7d8 piercing** on failure, half on success. **At Higher Levels.** +1d8 per slot above 6th.

## Wind Walk
*6th-level transmutation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 minute | **Range:** 30 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (fire and holy water) | **Duration:** 8 hours
You and up to ten willing creatures within range assume gaseous form for the duration, appearing as wisps of cloud. In cloud form, a creature has a **flying speed of 300 feet** and has resistance to damage from nonmagical weapons. A creature can revert to normal form (1 minute to change), and can become cloud form again (1 minute to change). While in cloud form, a creature can't attack, cast spells, or manipulate objects.

## Word of Recall
*6th-level conjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 5 feet | **Components:** V | **Duration:** Instantaneous
You and up to five willing creatures within 5 feet of you instantly teleport to a previously designated sanctuary. You and any creatures that teleport with you appear in the nearest unoccupied space to the spot you designated when you prepared your sanctuary (requires a 10-minute casting of this spell at the location).
