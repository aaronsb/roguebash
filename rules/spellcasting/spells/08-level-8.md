# 8th-Level Spells

## Animal Shapes
*8th-level transmutation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 30 feet | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 24 hours
Choose any number of willing creatures that you can see within range. You transform each one into a Large or smaller beast with a CR of 4 or lower. On subsequent turns, you can use your action to transform affected creatures into new forms. The transformation lasts for the duration for each target, or until the target drops to 0 HP or dies. You can choose different beast forms for each target. A target's game statistics are replaced by the beast's, but it retains its alignment and INT, WIS, and CHA scores. Excess damage carries over when the creature reverts.

## Antimagic Field
*8th-level abjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** Self (10-foot-radius sphere) | **Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of powdered iron or iron filings) | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour
A 10-foot-radius invisible sphere of antimagic surrounds you. Within the sphere: spells can't be cast, summoned creatures temporarily disappear, magic items become mundane, magical effects are suppressed. Spells and magical effects that originate outside the sphere can't affect anything within it. Targeted spells that enter the area are lost. Areas of effect that include the sphere are suppressed within it. A creature's innate magical traits are suppressed. Artifacts and deities are unaffected.

## Antipathy/Sympathy
*8th-level enchantment*
- **Casting Time:** 1 hour | **Range:** 60 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (a lump of alum soaked in vinegar for the antipathy effect, or a drop of honey for the sympathy effect) | **Duration:** 10 days
This spell attracts or repels creatures of your choice. Choose a Huge or smaller object, creature, or area (up to 200-foot cube) within range. Then specify a kind of creature. **Antipathy**: creatures of the specified kind must succeed on a WIS save or become frightened when within 60 feet of the target and must use movement to move to the nearest safe spot farther than 60 feet away. **Sympathy**: creatures must succeed on a WIS save or use movement each turn to move toward the target. Once within 5 feet, the creature can't willingly move away.

## Clone
*8th-level necromancy*
- **Casting Time:** 1 hour | **Range:** Touch | **Components:** V, S, M (a diamond worth at least 1,000 gp and at least 1 cubic inch of flesh of the creature to be cloned, consumed, plus a vessel worth at least 2,000 gp with a sealable lid and large enough to hold a Medium creature) | **Duration:** Instantaneous
This spell grows an inert duplicate of a living creature as a safeguard against death. The clone forms inside a sealed vessel and grows to full size and maturity after 120 days. At any time after the clone matures, if the original creature dies, its soul transfers to the clone (if the soul is free and willing). The clone is physically identical to the original and has the same personality, memories, and abilities, but none of the original's equipment.

## Control Weather
*8th-level transmutation*
- **Casting Time:** 10 minutes | **Range:** Self (5-mile radius) | **Components:** V, S, M (burning incense and bits of earth and wood mixed in water) | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 8 hours
You take control of the weather within 5 miles of you for the duration. You must be outdoors. You can change precipitation, temperature, and wind. It takes 1d4 x 10 minutes for new conditions to take effect. You can change conditions again; new conditions take another 1d4 x 10 minutes.

## Demiplane
*8th-level conjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 60 feet | **Components:** S | **Duration:** 1 hour
You create a shadowy door on a flat solid surface that you can see within range. The door is large enough to allow Medium creatures to pass through. When opened, it leads to a demiplane that appears to be an empty room 30 feet in each dimension, made of wood or stone. When the spell ends, the door disappears. Creatures and objects inside the demiplane remain trapped there. Each time you cast this spell, you can create a new demiplane or connect to one you previously created.

## Dominate Monster
*8th-level enchantment*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 60 feet | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour
You attempt to beguile a creature. The target must succeed on a WIS save or be charmed. You have a telepathic link with it. You can use this link to issue commands to the creature (no action required by you). Each time the target takes damage, it makes a new WIS save against the spell. On success, the spell ends. **At Higher Levels.** 9th: concentration up to 8 hours.

## Earthquake
*8th-level evocation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 500 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of dirt, a piece of rock, and a lump of clay) | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
You create a seismic disturbance at a point on the ground within range. For the duration, an intense tremor rips through the ground in a 100-foot-radius circle centered on that point. The ground is difficult terrain. Each creature on the ground that is concentrating must make a CON save or lose concentration. At the end of each turn you spend concentrating, each creature on the ground must make a DEX save or be knocked prone. Fissures open (1d10 x 10 feet deep, 10 feet wide). Structures: DEX save DC 15, 5d6 bludgeoning damage on failure, collapses if reduced to 0 HP.

## Feeblemind
*8th-level enchantment*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 150 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (a handful of clay, crystal, glass, or mineral spheres) | **Duration:** Instantaneous
You blast the mind of a creature. The target must make an INT save: **4d6 psychic** on failure. On failure, the creature's **INT and CHA scores become 1**. The creature can't cast spells, activate magic items, understand language, or communicate in any intelligible way. The creature can, however, identify its friends, follow them, and even protect them. The creature can repeat the save every 30 days. Greater Restoration, Heal, or Wish ends it.

## Glibness
*8th-level transmutation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** Self | **Components:** V | **Duration:** 1 hour
Until the spell ends, when you make a CHA check, you can replace the number you roll with a 15. Additionally, no matter what you say, magic that would determine if you are telling the truth indicates that you are being truthful.

## Holy Aura
*8th-level abjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** Self | **Components:** V, S, M (a tiny reliquary worth at least 1,000 gp containing a sacred relic) | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
Divine light washes out from you in a 30-foot radius. Creatures of your choice in that radius when you cast the spell have advantage on all saving throws, and other creatures have disadvantage on attack rolls against them. When a fiend or an undead hits an affected creature with a melee attack, the aura flashes. The attacker must succeed on a CON save or be blinded until the spell ends.

## Incendiary Cloud
*8th-level conjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 150 feet | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
A swirling cloud of smoke shot through with white-hot embers appears in a 20-foot-radius sphere centered on a point within range. The cloud spreads around corners and is heavily obscured. It moves 10 feet away from you at the start of each of your turns. A creature entering or starting its turn there must make a DEX save: **10d8 fire** on failure, half on success.

## Maze
*8th-level conjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 60 feet | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes
You banish a creature that you can see within range into a labyrinthine demiplane. The target remains there for the duration or until it escapes. The target can use its action to attempt to escape by making a **DC 20 Intelligence check**. When it succeeds, it reappears in the space it left (or the nearest unoccupied space). When the spell ends, the target reappears in the space it left. A minotaur or goristro demon automatically succeeds.

## Mind Blank
*8th-level abjuration*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** Touch | **Components:** V, S | **Duration:** 24 hours
One willing creature you touch is immune to psychic damage, any effect that would sense its emotions or read its thoughts, divination spells, and the charmed condition. Even a Wish spell or effects of similar power can't affect the target's mind or gain information about the target.

## Power Word Stun
*8th-level enchantment*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 60 feet | **Components:** V | **Duration:** Instantaneous
You speak a word of power that can overwhelm the mind of one creature you can see within range. If the target has **150 hit points or fewer**, it is **stunned**. Otherwise, the spell has no effect. The stunned target must make a CON save at the end of each of its turns. On a successful save, the stunned effect ends.

## Sunburst
*8th-level evocation*
- **Casting Time:** 1 action | **Range:** 150 feet | **Components:** V, S, M (fire and a piece of sunstone) | **Duration:** Instantaneous
Brilliant sunlight flashes in a 60-foot radius centered on a point you choose. Each creature in that light must make a CON save: **12d6 radiant** on failure (half on success), and is **blinded for 1 minute** on failure. A blinded creature makes a CON save at the end of each of its turns, ending the blindness on success. Undead and oozes have disadvantage on the save. This spell dispels any darkness in its area that was created by a spell.
