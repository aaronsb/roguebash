## Notes on Spell School Assignments
- Some spells have had their school changed between printings. This index follows the SRD 5.1 assignments.
- Spells named after characters (e.g., Arcane Sword for Mordenkainen's Sword) use their SRD names.
- Subclass and domain spells that are not on the base class list are still listed under their school above; check the class index in `02-spell-index-by-class.md` for which classes can access each spell.
