## Spellcasting Focuses

**Arcane Focus.** A crystal, orb, rod, staff, or wand. Replaces non-costed material components for arcane casters.

| Focus | Cost | Weight |
|-------|------|--------|

**Druidic Focus.** A sprig of mistletoe, totem, wooden staff, or yew wand. Replaces non-costed material components for druids.

**Holy Symbol.** An amulet, emblem, or reliquary. Replaces non-costed material components for clerics and paladins.

## Special Items

**Acid.** As an action, splash on a creature within 5 feet or throw up to 20 feet. Ranged attack against a creature or object. On hit, 2d6 acid damage.

**Alchemist's Fire.** As an action, throw up to 20 feet. Ranged attack. On hit, 1d4 fire damage at start of each turn. A creature can end this damage by using its action to make a DC 10 Dexterity check to extinguish.

**Antitoxin.** A creature that drinks this vial gains advantage on saving throws against being poisoned for 1 hour.

**Ball Bearings.** As an action, spill on level ground covering a 10-foot square. A creature moving through the area must succeed on a DC 10 Dexterity save or fall prone.

**Caltrops.** As an action, spread to cover a 5-foot square. Creature entering makes DC 15 Dexterity save or stop and take 1 piercing damage, reducing speed by 10 feet until it regains at least 1 HP.

**Climber's Kit.** Includes pitons, boot tips, gloves, and a harness. You can anchor yourself so you can't fall more than 25 feet.

**Healer's Kit.** 10 uses. As an action, spend one use to stabilize a creature at 0 HP without needing a Medicine check.

**Holy Water.** As an action, splash on a creature within 5 feet or throw up to 20 feet. Ranged attack against fiend or undead. On hit, 2d6 radiant damage.

**Hunting Trap.** Set as an action. DC 13 Dexterity save or take 1d4 piercing damage and stop moving. DC 13 Strength check to free.

**Manacles.** DC 20 Dexterity check to escape. DC 20 Strength check to break. AC 19, 15 HP.

**Oil.** Splash on a creature within 5 feet or throw up to 20 feet. If ignited, takes 5 fire damage at start of each turn for 2 rounds. Poured on ground covers 5-foot square; if ignited burns 2 rounds dealing 5 fire to creature entering or ending turn.

**Ram, Portable.** Grants +4 bonus to Strength checks to break down doors. Another character can help for advantage.
