## Mounted Combat

While mounted, you can either control the mount or allow it to act independently.

**Controlled Mount.** Acts on your initiative. Can move and take the Dash, Disengage, or Dodge action only. It can move and act even on the turn you mount it.

**Independent Mount.** Retains its own initiative and full range of actions. It may flee from combat, rush to attack, or act as its nature directs.

**Forced Dismount.** If an effect moves your mount against its will, you must succeed on a DC 10 Dexterity saving throw or fall off the mount, landing prone in a space within 5 feet. If you're knocked prone while mounted, you make the same save. If your mount is knocked prone, you can use your reaction to dismount and land on your feet, otherwise you are dismounted and fall prone in a space within 5 feet.
