## AC Calculation

Your base AC depends on what armor you are wearing:

- **No armor**: 10 + DEX modifier
- **Light armor**: Armor's base + DEX modifier
- **Medium armor**: Armor's base + DEX modifier (max 2)
- **Heavy armor**: Armor's base AC (no DEX)
- **Shield**: +2 to AC (can be combined with armor)

### Unarmored Defense Variants
- **Barbarian**: 10 + DEX modifier + CON modifier (no armor, shields OK)
- **Monk**: 10 + DEX modifier + WIS modifier (no armor, no shield)

## Armor Proficiency

If you wear armor you lack proficiency with, you have disadvantage on any ability check, saving throw, or attack roll that involves Strength or Dexterity, and you can't cast spells.

## Strength Requirement

If the armor table shows a Strength requirement, the armor reduces your speed by 10 feet unless you meet the requirement.

## Stealth

If the armor table shows "Disadvantage" in the Stealth column, you have disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks.
