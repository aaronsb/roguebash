## Carrying Capacity

- **Carrying Capacity**: STR score x 15 pounds
- **Push, Drag, or Lift**: STR score x 30 pounds (speed drops to 5 feet if over carrying capacity)

### Variant: Encumbrance
- **Encumbered** (over 5 x STR): Speed reduced by 10 feet
- **Heavily Encumbered** (over 10 x STR): Speed reduced by 20 feet, disadvantage on ability checks, attack rolls, and saving throws that use Strength, Dexterity, or Constitution

### Size and Carrying Capacity
- **Tiny**: Halve carrying capacity
- **Large**: Double carrying capacity
- **Huge**: Quadruple carrying capacity
- **Gargantuan**: Multiply by 8
